Nothing To Do - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Nothing To Do! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app.

Information We Don't Collect

Nothing To Do is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data (including your streaks and notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your daily check-ins
  • Optional notes you add to your check-ins
  • App preferences (such as language settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app will send you local reminders. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features. The app uses standard iOS data protection mechanisms to ensure your information remains secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
